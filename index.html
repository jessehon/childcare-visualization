Affordability in Major Regions

Child care affordability is measured in the average price that is
paid in each region for an hour of childcare.

This can range from $6 to $18.

The highest priced regions in the country cluster
around Sydney inner suburbs like Leichhardt, Eastern Suburbs, Mosman,
and Ku-ring-gai.

A path forward

Fundamental demographic shifts are going to continue
to make child care challenging in the future.

One of the most stressful questions a new parent confronts is,
"Who's going to take care of my baby when I go back to work?"
